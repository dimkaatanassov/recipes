Ingredients:
- Two large tomatoes
- Two persian cucumbers
- One geen onion
- A tea spoon of olive oil
- Half a tea spoon of salt

Directions:
1. Wash thoroighly the tomatoes, cucumbers and green onion
2. Chop the tomatoes into cubes and place in a bowl
3. Chop the cucumbers into cubes and add to bowl
4. Chop the onion into small circilse and add to bowl
5. Add olive oil
6. Add salt
7. Mix and serve
